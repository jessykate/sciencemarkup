Science Markup

machine-readable metadata for online science content.

21st century science is happening at smaller units of discourse, in the open, with communities of peers. Join the conversation, take your work further, and make sure you get credit.

Discoverable

Markup makes the key salient parts of your work stand out from the other content, making it easier for automated scripts and other tools to identify, highlight, and even index your content for others, without any work on the part of the author.
